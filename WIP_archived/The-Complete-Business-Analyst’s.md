## Technique 2: Research Prompt Generation

**The Problem**: As a BA, you often need deep research on topics you’re unfamiliar with (APIs, technical feasibility, market analysis).

**The Solution**: Mary generates focused research prompts you can use with ChatGPT, Claude, or Perplexity.

**Command**:

```
*research-prompt TikTok trend collection
```

**Mary’s Complete Research Prompt** (copy this directly into ChatGPT):

```
Deep Research Prompt: Technical Methods for TikTok Trend Collection
Research Objective
To identify, analyze, and recommend the most effective and reliable technical 
method for collecting trend data from TikTok. The research must determine the 
feasibility, cost, scalability, and limitations of each potential approach to 
inform the core architecture of the "PromoCraft" MVP.
Background Context
The project requires building a "TikTok trend collector" as a core feature. 
The viability and development timeline of this feature are critically dependent 
on the technical method chosen for data acquisition.
Research Questions
Primary Questions (Must Answer)
1. Official TikTok API: Does TikTok provide a formal API for accessing data on 
   trends, videos, hashtags, and sounds? What are the access requirements, rate 
   limits, costs, and specific terms of service?
2. Third-Party Data Providers: Who are the leading third-party services that 
   provide TikTok data/analytics via an API? Provide a comparison based on pricing, 
   data freshness, breadth of data points, and limitations.
3. Web Scraping Feasibility: What are the current techniques for scraping TikTok? 
   What are the primary technical challenges and associated legal and ethical risks?
Expected Deliverables
- Executive Summary: A clear, data-backed recommendation for the single best data 
  collection method for the MVP
- Detailed Analysis: Official API Report, Third-Party Provider Comparison, 
  Scraping Feasibility Report
- Supporting Materials: List of all URLs for APIs, data providers, and key articles
Success Criteria
The research is considered successful if it delivers a clear, actionable 
recommendation that enables the development team to immediately begin designing 
and building the trend collector with a well-defined technical strategy.
```

**Result**: When used with ChatGPT, this prompt generated a comprehensive 118-line research document with executive summary, comparison tables, decision matrix, and implementation timeline — exactly what you need for technical planning.

```
Simulated Stakeholder Round Table

1. Victoria, the Investor:
"The phased timeline is an improvement, but what's the total estimated 
time-to-market in weeks? The constraint to 'minimize reliance on paid APIs' 
is wise, but what is the actual monthly budget we're allocating for 
'cost-effective third-party providers'? We need to put a number on that."
2. Alex, the Technical Lead:
"The plan's biggest vulnerability is the approval time for the official TikTok 
APIs. If that takes weeks, Phase 2 is dead in the water. We need to de-risk

official application."
3. David, the Marketing Manager:
"This brief is technically sound, but who are we marketing the MVP to on day one? 
The success metrics are good, but they're meaningless without an acquisition plan.

1. De-risk the Timeline: Development should begin using third-party API immediately
2. Add a Budget: Specific monthly budget for third-party APIs needed
3. Define Go-To-Market: New section needed for user acquisition strategy
```

**Result**: Mary identified three critical gaps that would have caused major issues during development and launch.
